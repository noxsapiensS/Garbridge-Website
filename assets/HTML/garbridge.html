ABOUT THE COMPANY

Key values of our company

Convenience

We strive to ensure a convenient transition to our services for companies recycling their waste, and companies trying to repurpose the waste we collect.

Passion

We're passionate about solving food waste and promoting the economic transition into greener energy.

Competitive

We ensure competitive but accessible pricing for our services.

Supportive

We have a dedicated support team that answer questions and make arrangements about our business 24/7.

We know waste management.

Our company collects relevant food waste from supermarkets, restaurants, shops and parties
supply bio-fuel companies with bio materials, contributing to a wasteless society and circular economy.

Alongside this we also provide a platform for customers to arrange their relevant food waste
to be picked up by us and contribute to an eco friendly society.
